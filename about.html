About Us

We are Group 23 from Swinburne University of Technology, Sarawak Campus. Our team developed this dashboard as part of the COS30045 Data Visualisation Project.

This website visualises mobile phone enforcement trends in Australia from 2008–2024 using D3.js. Our goal is to transform raw enforcement data into clear, interactive, and meaningful insights that help users:

  • Compare Enforcement Trends Across Jurisdiction
  • Analyse the Impact of Detection Methods Over Time
  • Identify Outliers and High-Performance Jurisdictions
  • Focus on Specific Time Periods for Coordinated Analysis
  • Explore Detailed Data On-Demand for specific Data Points

This project reflects our commitment to clean design, usability, and analytical storytelling. Thank you for exploring our work.
